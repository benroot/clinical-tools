Pregnancy Due Date Calculator

Enter the first day of the last menstrual period (LMP) to estimate the due date.

Format: MM/DD/YYYY — or enter an offset from today, like w-6 (6 weeks ago) or t-45 (45 days ago). The field will be replaced with the resulting date once you leave it.

Estimated due date

Estimate based on Naegele's Rule (LMP + 280 days / 40 weeks), which assumes a 28-day cycle. A care provider may adjust this based on ultrasound dating or a known cycle length.
